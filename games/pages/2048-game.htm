How to play:
  • Left, Right, Up, Down
  • Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When three tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!
Tips and tricks:
  • Understand how the board moves. In 2048 you only have four main gestures: up, down, left, and right. ...
  • Don't chase large tiles. ...
  • Work your way towards the corners. ...
  • Plan ahead. ...
  • Slow down and think. ...
  • Always make moves where multiple tiles merge first.
  • Merge: +1 +1 -> +2
  • Merge: 2 3 4 (bonus)
